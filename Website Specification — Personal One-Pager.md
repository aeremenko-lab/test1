## 4) Visual & Interaction Spec (Plain Language)

### Layout & Spacing

- Single-column, editorial flow
- Generous whitespace
- Clear typographic hierarchy
- Content breathes; nothing feels crowded

### Typography Feel

- Serif-forward, newspaper-inspired
- Strong headings, calm body text
- Visual authority through rhythm and alignment

### Color Palette

- Light neutral background (off-white / paper tone)
- Dark text (near-black)
- One restrained accent color for links or CTA only

### Photo Treatment

- One candid, natural portrait
- Integrated into layout, not dominant
- Editorial crop, calm expression

### Buttons & Links

- Text-first buttons
- Understated hover states
- No flashy effects

### Motion Principles

- Minimal and subtle
- Gentle fade or reveal on scroll
- No animation that distracts from reading

### Mobile Behavior

- Same editorial hierarchy
- Excellent readability
- CTA always accessible without feeling intrusive

## 5) Acceptance Checklist (Testable)

- Site feels premium and restrained
- Tone matches investor / board expectations
- Content is clear within 30 seconds of scanning
- Smooth, unobtrusive scrolling
- Mobile experience is equally authoritative
- CTA is visible but never aggressive
- No placeholders, filler text, or invented claims
